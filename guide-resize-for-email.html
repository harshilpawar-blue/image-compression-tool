How to Resize Photos for Email

Have you ever tried to email photos to family, only to be told the files are "too big"? Most email services like Gmail or Outlook have a 25MB limit.

Modern cameras take huge pictures. A single photo can be 10MB or more. This means you can only send 2 or 3 photos at a time.

The Solution:
Make the dimensions smaller. A photo does not need to be huge to look good on a screen.

Step-by-Step Resize Guide

  1. Upload: Open our Image Resizer tool and pick your photos.
  2. Choose Size: Select "By Percentage" and choose "50%". This will cut the size in half.
  3. Process: Click "Resize Images".
  4. Send: Download the new files. They will now attach to your email instantly.

Your family will still see the photos clearly, but they will download much faster.
