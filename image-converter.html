Why convert image formats?

Not all image formats are created equal. Different devices and websites require specific file types to display images correctly. Our tool helps you bridge the gap by converting modern formats like HEIC or WebP into universally accepted formats like JPG.

Understanding the Formats

JPG (JPEG)

Small file size. Best for photographs. Does not support transparency.

PNG

High quality. Supports transparent backgrounds. Larger file size.

WEBP

Modern format for websites. High quality and tiny file size.

How to use this converter

  1. Upload: Select images from your device. We support PNG, BMP, GIF, WEBP, and TIFF.
  2. Choose Format: Select your desired output (JPG, PNG, or WEBP) from the dropdown menu.
  3. Convert: Click the button to process your files.
  4. Download: Your images will be downloaded automatically. If you converted multiple files, they will be zipped for convenience.

Privacy Guarantee

Unlike other services that upload your private photos to a remote cloud server, The Image Toolkit processes your files entirely within your browser. Your photos never leave your device, ensuring maximum privacy and security.
