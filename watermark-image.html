Free Watermark Tool

Protect your digital assets by adding a watermark. Whether it's your name, website URL, or company logo, placing a watermark ensures viewers know who the image belongs to.

Why use this tool?

  • Dual Modes: Type text directly or upload a transparent PNG logo.
  • Full Control: Adjust opacity to make the watermark subtle but visible.
  • Smart Positioning: Instantly place your mark in any corner or the center with one click.
  • Secure: Everything happens in your browser. Your original image is never sent to a server.
