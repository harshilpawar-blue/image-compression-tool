Best Image Sizes for Instagram & Facebook

Using the wrong image size on social media makes your photos look blurry or cropped. To look professional, you must resize your images to the exact dimensions each platform prefers.

Instagram Dimensions

  • Square Post: 1080 x 1080 pixels
  • Portrait Post: 1080 x 1350 pixels (Best for visibility)
  • Stories / Reels: 1080 x 1920 pixels

Facebook Dimensions

  • Profile Picture: 170 x 170 pixels
  • Cover Photo: 820 x 312 pixels
  • Shared Image: 1200 x 630 pixels
Pro Tip:
Always resize your image *before* uploading. If you let Instagram resize it for you, their automatic compression often lowers the quality.
