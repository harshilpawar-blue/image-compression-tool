Why compress your images?

High-resolution images are heavy. A single photo from a modern smartphone can be over 5MB. This causes slow website loading times, high data usage for mobile visitors, and difficulty sharing files via email.

Image compression solves this by intelligently removing data that the human eye cannot see. The result is a much smaller file that looks nearly identical to the original.

Lossy vs Lossless Compression

Our tool uses smart lossy compression. This means we slightly reduce the color precision of the image. This technique is undetectable to the human eye but can reduce file sizes by up to 90%.

Features

  • Target File Size: Need a photo under 50KB for a job application or visa form? Use our "Target Size" tab to specify the exact limit.
  • Bulk Processing: Compress dozens of images at once.
  • Privacy: We use Client-Side processing. Your images are compressed locally on your computer, never sent to a cloud server.

How it improves SEO

Google considers "Page Speed" a ranking factor. Large images are the #1 cause of slow websites. By compressing your images before uploading them to WordPress or Shopify, you significantly improve your Core Web Vitals score.
