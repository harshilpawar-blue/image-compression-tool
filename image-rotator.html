Rotate and Straighten Images Online

Have you ever taken a photo on your phone only to find it uploaded sideways? Or scanned a document that is slightly crooked? The Image Toolkit's rotation tool helps you fix image orientation instantly.

Features

  • 90-Degree Rotation: Quickly turn images from portrait to landscape (or vice versa) with a single click.
  • Precision Straightening: Use the custom angle slider to correct crooked horizons by 1 or 2 degrees.
  • Smart Canvas resizing: When you rotate an image by 45 degrees, the corners would usually be cut off. Our tool automatically expands the canvas so you never lose a pixel.

Why use this tool?

Most operating systems have basic rotation viewers, but they don't always save the changes to the file's metadata permanently. By processing your image here and downloading it, the new orientation is "baked in" to the pixels, ensuring it looks correct on every device and website you upload it to.

Privacy First

Just like our other tools, rotation happens inside your web browser using HTML5 Canvas technology. We do not upload your pictures to a server to rotate them.
