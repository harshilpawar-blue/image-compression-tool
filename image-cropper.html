How to Crop Images Online

Our free online image cropper allows you to easily cut out unneeded parts of your photos. It's perfect for framing a subject, changing the aspect ratio for social media (like Instagram squares or YouTube thumbnails), or focusing on a specific detail.

Quick Steps

  1. Upload your image.
  2. Drag the corners of the selection box on the image to define the area you want to keep.
  3. Use the buttons on the right to lock a specific aspect ratio if needed.
  4. Click "Crop Image" to process and download your new picture.
