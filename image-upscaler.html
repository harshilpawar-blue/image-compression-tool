Enhance Image Quality for Free

Low-resolution images look blurry and pixelated when you try to print them or view them on modern HD screens. The Image Toolkit's Upscaler allows you to increase image dimensions while maintaining smoothness.

How does it work?

Unlike basic resizing which stretches pixels (making them look like blocks), our tool uses Smart Bicubic Smoothing. This algorithm analyzes the pixels and interpolates (estimates) new data between them to create smooth edges and curves.

Features

  • 2x & 4x Modes: Double or quadruple your image size instantly.
  • No Pixelation: Smooths out jagged edges common in low-res photos.
  • Comparison Slider: See the quality difference immediately with our Before/After view.
  • Secure: All processing happens in your browser. We never see your photos.

When to use this tool?

  • Printing: Ensure your photos look sharp on paper.
  • Web Design: Create high-resolution assets for Retina displays.
  • Restoration: Improve old, small digital photos.
