Split PDF Files Instantly

The Image Toolkit's PDF Splitter allows you to extract specific pages from a document or separate every page into its own file. It's fast, private, and works entirely in your browser.

Two Ways to Split

  • Extract Mode: Select pages (e.g., 1, 3, 5) and save them as a single new PDF document. Ideal for removing unwanted pages.
  • Separate Mode: Save every selected page as an individual PDF file. Perfect for breaking apart scanned batches.
