Convert PDF to Word for Free

Editing a PDF file can be difficult without expensive software. The Image Toolkit solves this by extracting the content from your PDF and creating a Microsoft Word compatible file (.doc) that you can edit easily.

How it works

We analyze the text layers within your PDF document. Our algorithm identifies paragraphs, lines, and breaks, then reconstructs this text into a format that word processors understand. This works best for Native PDFs (documents created from Word, Google Docs, etc.).

Important Note on Scanned Files

If your PDF is a scanned image (a photo of a document), standard conversion cannot extract editable text because there is no text layer. For scanned files, you would need OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology, which this tool does not currently support.

Privacy First

Most online converters require you to upload your file to their server. We are different. Our converter runs inside your browser. Your confidential contracts, CVs, or financial documents never leave your computer.
