Convert PDF to Editable Word

Turn your read-only PDF files into editable Word documents with The Image Toolkit. Our converter extracts text and paragraphs, creating a file you can edit in Microsoft Word, Google Docs, or OpenOffice.

How it works

  • Text Extraction: We scan the PDF text layers to retrieve content.
  • Layout Preservation: We attempt to keep paragraphs and structure intact.
  • Secure: Conversion happens in your browser. Your confidential documents are never uploaded to any server.
