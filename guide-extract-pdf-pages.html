How to Extract Specific Pages from a PDF

Sometimes you download a 50-page report, but you only need page 10. Sending the whole file is unnecessary and wastes storage space.

You can use a "Splitter" tool to pull out just the pages you want.

Why split a PDF?

  • Remove Clutter: Delete blank pages or cover sheets you do not need.
  • Share Only What Matters: Send just the invoice page, not the whole contract.
  • Smaller Files: A 1-page PDF is much smaller than a 50-page one.

How to Extract Pages

  1. Upload: Go to our PDF Splitter and open your document.
  2. Select Pages: You will see a preview of every page. Click the ones you want to keep.
  3. Choose Mode: Select "Extract" to combine them into one new file.
  4. Download: Get your new, lightweight PDF instantly.
