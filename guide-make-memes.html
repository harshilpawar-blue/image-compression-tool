How to Make Your Own Memes

Memes are the language of the internet. The most popular format is an image with bold white text at the top and bottom.

You don't need to be a graphic designer to make one. You can create a meme in seconds using your own photos.

What makes a good meme?

  • Relatable: It should be something many people have experienced.
  • Short Text: Keep it punchy. Too much reading ruins the joke.
  • Clear Font: The standard font is "Impact" because it is easy to read on any background.
Tip: Use a photo of your pet or a funny moment with friends to make it unique!

Create one in 3 steps

  1. Open Generator: Go to our Meme Generator tool.
  2. Upload: Pick your funny image.
  3. Add Text: Type your joke in the Top and Bottom boxes. The tool handles the font and outline for you.
