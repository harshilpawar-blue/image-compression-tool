Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 24, 2025

1. Introduction

ToolKit Pro ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website.

We do not require you to create an account to use our tools. You can use our services anonymously.

2. User Files and Data Handling

Our core service involves processing files (images and PDFs) that you upload. We treat your files with strict confidentiality.

  • Temporary Storage: We upload your files to our secure servers solely to process them (e.g., to compress or convert).
  • Automatic Deletion: We delete all uploaded and processed files from our servers automatically after one (1) hour. We do not keep backups.
  • No Ownership: We claim no ownership over your content. You retain all rights to your files.
  • Local Processing: Where possible, we process files directly in your web browser. In these cases, your files never leave your device.

3. Information We Collect

We collect limited information to maintain our site performance and security.

  • Log Data: We log standard technical data, such as your IP address, browser type, and access times. This helps us detect abuse and fix errors.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to improve your experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some site features may not work correctly.

4. Third-Party Services and Ads

We use third-party services to support our free platform.

Google AdSense: We display ads provided by Google. Google uses cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This tool collects anonymous data, such as page views and session duration.

5. Data Security

We protect your data using industry standards. We use SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encryption for all data transfer between your device and our servers. We limit access to our servers to authorized personnel only.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you have rights regarding your data. These may include the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Since we do not store user files permanently or require accounts, we hold very little personal data. If you have questions about your data, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will post the new date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@toolkitpro.com
