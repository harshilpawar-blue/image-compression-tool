Combine PDF Files for Free

Merging PDFs used to require expensive software like Adobe Acrobat. The Image Toolkit changes that by providing a powerful, free, and secure way to combine documents directly in your browser.

Two Ways to Merge

  1. File Mode (Simple): Upload multiple files and stack them on top of each other. Great for combining a cover letter and a CV.
  2. Page Mode (Advanced): This mode breaks your PDFs apart into individual pages. You can then drag and drop specific pages to reorder them, delete unwanted pages, or mix pages from different files into a new document.

Use Cases

  • Business: Combine monthly reports, invoices, and receipts into a single file for accounting.
  • Education: Merge lecture notes, assignment criteria, and your research into one study guide.
  • Legal: Stitch together scanned contract pages that were saved as separate files.

Security Guarantee

We use Client-Side Processing. This means the merging process happens on your computer using JavaScript libraries. Your sensitive documents are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring 100% privacy.
